About Us

Anicca Holdings LLC is a Delaware-based consulting, management, and investing company focused on supporting businesses and investors across Latin America and the United States. We provide strategic advisory, transaction support, and business development services with a cross-border perspective.

Founded with the vision of bridging markets, our approach combines rigorous analysis with hands-on operational knowledge to deliver results that are sustainable, compliant, and growth-oriented.

What We Do

  • M&A Advisory & Structuring – Advising on cross-border mergers, acquisitions, and divestitures, with a focus on maximizing value.
  • Business Development – Identifying and introducing strategic clients, partners, and investors throughout the U.S. and Latin America.
  • Management Consulting – Providing operational and financial advisory to support expansion, restructuring, and performance improvement.
  • Investing – Deploying capital selectively into opportunities in Latin America and the U.S., with a focus on strategic, long-term value creation.

Our Approach

  • Independent – We are not tied to a single fund, bank, or group, ensuring full alignment with client outcomes.
  • Cross-Border Expertise – Deep understanding of U.S. and Latin American business landscape.
  • Hands-On – We don’t just advise; we help implement structures and processes that endure.
  • Trusted Network – Long-standing relationships with entrepreneurs, family offices, and corporates across multiple geographies.
