About Anicca Holdings

Anicca Holdings LLC is a Delaware-based consulting, management, and investing company focused on supporting businesses and investors across Latin America and the United States.

We provide strategic advisory, transaction support, and business development services with a cross-border perspective.

Founded with the vision of bridging markets, our approach combines rigorous analysis with hands-on operational knowledge to deliver results that are sustainable, compliant, and growth-oriented.
